Why templates?
--------------

Our research shows that a lot of cities have similar problems that have solutions scattered across many agencies and nonprofits.
StartHereCMS was created to make creating guides for your citizens as easy and cheap as possible.

Templates allow you to quickly create some pages using a simple heroku run command.
Simply open the console in your app's dashboard on Heroku and enter the template name you want:
